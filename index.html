Legend.Dev Goals

Over the next two years, my primary goal is to transition from a front-end developer to a proficient full stack developer specializing in the MERN stack. Having spent a year and a half honing my skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React, I am ready to embark on this journey. I plan to start working soon, leveraging my current expertise to secure a position that places me above the status of a junior developer.

In my role as a front-end developer, I aim to excel in my responsibilities, delivering high-quality work and collaborating effectively with my team. My objective is to contribute significantly to projects, showcasing my ability to handle complex tasks and demonstrate leadership potential. This experience will be instrumental as I gradually expand my skill set to include back-end technologies.

To master the MERN stack, I will dedicate time to learning MongoDB, Express.js, and Node.js, integrating these technologies with my existing knowledge of React. I plan to work on personal projects, contribute to open-source initiatives, and seek mentorship from experienced full stack developers. By doing so, I aim to build a solid understanding of full stack development, enabling me to tackle both front-end and back-end challenges.

In summary, my goals for the next two years are to excel as a front-end developer, elevate my status beyond junior level, and master the MERN stack. This journey will prepare me to become a versatile and valuable team player in the tech industry.
